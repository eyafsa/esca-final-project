Top Pick For Moisturizers

For Oily skin: Cosrx Oil-Free Ultra-Moisturizing Lotion with Birch Sap

This oil-free moisturizer has been the holy grail for my oily skin. Lots of facial lotions include multiple oils that can make the skin feel soft, but can be overwhelming for people who produce enough face oil on their own. Having hydrating Betula Platyphylla Japonica Juice as its main ingredient, this lotion doesn't add excess product on to our natural oils. With its birch sap that calms the skin and prevents redness, this is perfect for anyone who has irritated skin. It glides smoothly and absorbs quickly.

Perfect For:

  • Oily Skin
  • Normal Skin
  • Combination Skin

INGREDIENTS: Betula Platyphylla Japonica Juice, Butylene Glycol, Glycerin, Dimethicone, Betaine, Cetearyl Alcohol, 1,2-Hexanediol, Cetearyl Olivate, Sorbitan Olivate, Ethylhexylglycerin, Hydroxyethyl Acrylate/Sodium Acryloyldimethyl Taurate Copolymer, Ethyl Hexanediol, Xanthan Gum, Allantoin, Panthenol, Sodium Hyaluronate, Sodium Lactate, 4-Terpineol, Water Source: Cosrx.com


For Dry / Combination skin:
Tatcha - The Dewy Skin Cream

If you're willing to spend a little extra on yourself, this Tatcha cream is the way to go. Super moisturizing and smooth, this lotion gives the skin a simple but super hydrating feel. Its delicate texture sinks easily into the skin and gives a nice glow. Its use of hyaluronic acid adds an additional boost by plumping the skin and Tatchas iconic Japanese purple rice ingredient helps prevent any fine lines and wrinkles.

Perfect For:

  • Dry Skin
  • Normal Skin
  • Combination Skin

INGREDIENTS: Aqua/water/eau, Saccharomyces/rice Ferment Filtrate, Glycerin, Propanediol, Dimethicone, Squalane, Diisostearyl Malate, Behenyl Alcohol, Myristyl Myristate, Dipentaerythrityl Hexahydroxystearate, Bis-diglyceryl Polyacyladipate-2, Tridecyl Trimellitate, Inositol, Sorbitan Tristearate, Beheneth-20, Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract, Cladosiphon Okamuranus Extract, Chondrus Crispus Extract, Betaphycus Gelatinum Extract, Oryza Sativa (Rice) Bran Extract, Panax Ginseng Root Extract, Origanum Majorana Leaf Extract, Thymus Serpyllum Extract, Malva Sylvestris (Mallow) Flower Extract, Sodium Hyaluronate, Gold, Sericin, Phytosteryl Macadamiate, Tocopherol, Hydroxyethyl Acrylate/sodium Acryloyldimethyl Taurate Copolymer, Sorbitan Isostearate, Polysorbate 60, Butylene Glycol, Dimethicone/vinyl Dimethicone Crosspolymer, Sodium Benzoate, Gluconolactone, Ethylhexylglycerin, Parfum/fragrance, Alcohol, Phenoxyethanol, Citral, Limonene, Linalool, Mica, Tin Oxide, Titanium Dioxide (Ci 77891), Violet 2 (Ci 60725).
